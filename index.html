About Us

In celebration of White Day, CSUF SWE is hosting a unique designathon event aimed at bringing together like-minded individuals to explore coding, collaborate, and innovate. While our focus is on creating a space where women feel empowered, this event is open to all participants who are committed to fostering a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone.

Whether you’re an experienced coder or new to the world of tech, come chill, hack, and connect with others who share your passion for creativity and technology. Let’s build something meaningful together!

Prizes

Prizes will be given after the designathon during the closing ceremony.

FAQ

What is this designathon?

A designathon is a time-constrained event where participants collaborate to develop innovative design solutions for a specific challenge. While similar to a hackathon, it focuses primarily on design, creativity, and user experience.

Who can participate?

This event is open to all CSUF students.

Is there a participation fee?

No, participation is completely free of charge.

Do I need prior experience?

No, the event is open to individuals of all skill levels, including beginners.

Can I work on a project I started prior to the designathon?

No, all projects must be initiated at the start of the designathon. Pre-existing work is not permitted.

What should I bring?

Please bring yourself, water, your laptop, and a charger.

How can I stay updated?

Join our Discord community to receive updates and stay informed.

Who can I contact for more information?

For any additional questions, please contact us at swecsuf.president@gmail.com.
